FAQ

What is Kheyl
At Kheyl, pay a small entry fee to compete in a game-a-thon, where you can play multiple games for hours and vie for a prize pool that grows with every participant.
How does the game-a-thon work
Participants pay a fixed entry fee to join a game-a-thon that lasts for several hours, featuring a diverse selection of games. The exact duration and number of games will be specified in the event details.
How is the prize pool determined
The total prize pool is calculated by multiplying the number of participants by the entry fee. For example, if the entry fee is $20 and there are 50 participants, the prize pool will be $1000.
How are winners determined
Winners are determined based on their performance in the games played during the game-a-thon. Specific rules and scoring methods will be provided for each event.
Are there any additional costs during the game-a-thon
The entry fee covers participation in the game-a-thon. If participants wish to improve their score, they have the option to purchase additional games. However, to ensure fairness, the number of additional games available is limited.
How do I claim my prize if I win
Winners will be announced at the end of the game-a-thon. You will receive our "Koins," which can be redeemed directly at our store
